    Evaluating the Impact of an Income Shock on Crime: Evidence from the 2021 Child Tax Credit Expansion
    Co-authored with Shenghao Peng
    Working Paper

    This paper investigates how a temporary income shock brought about by the 2021 expansion of the U.S. Child Tax Credit (CTC) affects local crime rates.
